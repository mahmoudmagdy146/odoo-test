Point of Sales Extra Bank Charges Odoo Apps

POS - Point of Sales Bank Charges Odoo App is a valuable app to improve the process of including bank charges in your POS order payments. With this app, users gain the flexibility to activate the application of bank charges within their chosen payment methods. This ability to enable or disable bank charges adapts to their specific financial needs.

One of the app's best features is its ability to apply bank charges as a percentage of the transaction amount. This means that users can set the bank charge percentage according to their preferences. So, the app ensures that the correct amount of bank charges is accurately calculated and added to the transaction total.

When customers select a payment method that has been configured to include bank charges, the app automatically factors in these charges during the checkout process within the POS system. This automation eliminates manual calculations and helps maintain an efficient customer experience.
